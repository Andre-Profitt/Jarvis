JARVIS

Voice Interface

Click the microphone to start

How to use:

  1. Click the microphone button
  2. Say "Hey JARVIS" to activate
  3. Speak your command
  4. Your words will appear above
  5. Copy the text to paste into JARVIS

Voice Commands to Try:

  • "Hey JARVIS, what's the weather like?"
  • "Hey JARVIS, tell me about quantum computing"
  • "Hey JARVIS, write a Python function"
  • "Hey JARVIS, analyze my system status"
